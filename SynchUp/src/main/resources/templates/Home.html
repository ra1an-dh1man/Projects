About SynchUp

SynchUp is a modern, real-time chat application designed to make online conversations seamless, secure, and engaging. The app focuses on providing an intuitive user experience where people can connect instantly without worrying about delays or interruptions.

  • Real-Time Messaging: Messages are delivered instantly, ensuring smooth and live chat updates.
  • Secure Authentication: Firebase Authentication handles sign-up and login with strong security standards.
  • Typing Indicators: Know when your friend is typing in real-time, making conversations feel natural.
  • Clean & Responsive UI: lightweight and responsive front-end.
